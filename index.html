Our Services

Wedding Event Management

Canvas Events crafts elegant weddings with flawless planning, stunning décor, and seamless execution. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we bring your dream wedding to life, managing every detail so you can cherish every magical moment stress-free.

Engagement Planning

Celebrate your love story with a beautifully curated engagement. We design personalized themes, décor, and experiences, making your special day unforgettable. From venue styling to coordination, Canvas Events ensures your engagement reflects your personality and love.

Anniversary Party planning

Whether it’s your first or fiftieth, we create heartfelt anniversary celebrations filled with charm and elegance. From décor to entertainment, we make sure your milestone is unforgettable, celebrating love and memories in a truly special way.

Birthday & Baby showers

Make birthdays and baby showers extra special with unique themes, fun activities, and delightful décor. From kids’ parties to welcoming little ones, Canvas Events adds creativity and joy to every celebration, creating cherished moments with loved ones.

Corporate events

From conferences and seminars to product launches and annual meets, we plan corporate events with professionalism and creativity. Our services ensure flawless execution, impressive setups, and memorable experiences that strengthen your brand and delight your guests.

Event Co-ordination

Seamless coordination is at the heart of every successful event. Our expert team manages logistics, vendors, and timelines, ensuring your event flows smoothly. Canvas Events handles the details, so you enjoy stress-free, perfectly organized occasions.
